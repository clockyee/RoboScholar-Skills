# Search Playbook

## Privacy-Preserving Query Rules

- Use broad terms: field, method family, and journal names.
- Do not search unpublished title, abstract, exact hypotheses, exact result phrases, unique dataset names, or unique framework names.
- If the manuscript includes sensitive details, write private local notes and translate them into generic terms before browsing.

## Source Priority

1. Official journal/publisher pages for aims & scope, article types, author instructions, APCs, page limits, special issues, and calls for papers.
2. Clarivate JCR/Web of Science, institutional CAS tables, or publisher metric pages for SCI/SCIE, JCR quartile, and Journal Impact Factor.
3. IEEE/ACM/Springer/Elsevier/Wiley/SAGE/IOP/Mary Ann Liebert journal pages and society pages for field recognition and policies.
4. Reputable metric aggregators such as Bioxbio, JournalMetrics, LetPub, iJournal, or similar only when official/subscription sources are unavailable; cross-check when possible.
5. DOI landing pages, IEEE Xplore, ScienceDirect, SpringerLink, publisher pages, or Google Scholar-like public pages for similar articles.

## Query Patterns

- Journal metrics: `"<journal name>" "impact factor" "2024" "scope"`; `"<journal name>" "JCR" "Q1"`.
- CAS partition: `"<journal name>" "中科院分区" "2025"`; cross-check with at least two public sources when no institutional source exists.
- Scope: `"<journal name>" "aims and scope" official`.
- Author rules: `"<journal name>" "information for authors" "page limit"`.
- Similar articles: `site:<publisher-domain> "<journal name>" "<generic topic>"`.
- Special issues: `"<journal name>" "special issue" "submission deadline" "2026"` and the official special issue/call page.

## Evidence Checks

- Confirm article type compatibility: original research, review, letter, magazine article, methods, application, or special issue type.
- Confirm whether the venue accepts simulation-only work or expects physical systems.
- Confirm whether open access is optional or mandatory, and note APCs.
- For special issues, compare the deadline to the current date and mark expired calls clearly.
